About The Slough of Despond


Built in 1912, 235 Kensington Ave North was a church apparently built upon a muddy mire in East Hamilton. In the 1930s, a journalist for the Hamilton Herald called the building and congregation a "success", noting that it had been built from "the Slough of Despond".

In 2019, the church was purchased by Rachel and Daphne, and has become home to several artistic, musical, and community events.
